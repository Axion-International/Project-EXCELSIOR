One, what if the ruler of the universe is just a giant lizard with a volcano for a mouth and the second was," We should really try to figure out who has super powers" and allow them to manage their teams on the internet" He was very forward thinking. From that day till today Axion International has been the "go-to" company for all things related to tracking personal information of super heros.
